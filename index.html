About us

We at C.B.gym are an international sports community active since January 2023.

CBG Stands for “Casual Beginner’s Gym”.
We are always open for beginners who want to try out a sport.

What kind of sports do we play?

Touch Rugby

Touch Rugby is a non-contact derivative of Rugby in which players do not tackle, but rather touch opponents in order to gain possession of the ball.

Floorball

Floor ball, is a beginner friendly version of Floor Hockey. Instead of a puck, a lightweight plastic ball is used.
Contact is limited to a minimum and very easy to pick up.

Softball

Handball

A combination of basketball, waterpolo and soccer, handball is a team based sport, where the objective is to throw the ball into the opposing team's goal.
Players can take up to 3 steps and/or 30 seconds until they must pass or throw. With non-stop action, there is always something happening on the court!

Spikeball

Spikeball, also commonly known as Roundnet, is played between two teams.
The game starts with a serve from one team to another. Each team has up to three hits to return the ball to the net.
Points are scored when the ball hits the rim, the ground, bounces more than once on the net, etc.
