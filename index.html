What kind of sports do we play?

Floorball

Floorball or floor hockey is a team sport that is played indoors on a gym floor with specialized lightweight sticks and a plastic ball with holes.

Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate Frisbee, often referred to as just "Ultimate," is a fast-paced and a non-contact team sport played with a flying disc.
Each team defends one end zone. Points are scored when a player successfully passes the disc to a teammate in the opposite end zone.

Mölkky

Mölkky is a Finnish throwing game. The players knock over wooden pins, known as "skittles," by throwing a wooden stick, called a "mölkky," from a designated throwing line.
If a player knocks down one skittle, the player scores the number of points marked on that skittle.

Touch Rugby

Touch Rugby is a fast-paced and a limited-contact sport.
Like all forms of rugby, the aim is to score more tries than the opposition. A legal touch is on any part of the body, clothing and ball.
It is played by both men and women, this thrilling sport requires teamwork, skill, and quick thinking.

Spikeball

Spikeball, also commonly known as Roundnet, is played between two teams.
The game starts with a serve from one team to another. Each team has up to three hits to return the ball to the net.
Points are scored when the ball hits the rim, the ground, bounces more than once on the net, etc.
