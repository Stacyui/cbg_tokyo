Fujimidai JHS

 /富士見台中学校

Please enter through the western school gate.

Afterwards, enter the school building located on your right. Once inside, follow the corridor on your left until you reach a green mat.
Please take off your shoes and enter the gym, left of the green mat. Once inside, register at the stage.

If the gate is locked, or if there is anything you are unsure of, please contact the organizer through Meetup.
