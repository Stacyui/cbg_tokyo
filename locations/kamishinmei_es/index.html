Kamishinmei ES

 /上神明小学校

Please enter the western school gate.

Once you have entered through the gate, please turn right then head straight down the narrow path.
The gym is located at the end of the path.

If the gate is locked, please ring the bell, and ask the security to open the gate. Saying “Taikukan Desu” should suffice.
If the gate is still locked or there is no response, please contact the organizer through Meetup.
