Community plaza Yashio

 /コミュニティプラザ八潮

You can enter from either the northern or southern school gates.

Once you have entered through the gate, turn left, then head straight, where you will find the gym.

Please take off your shoes and place them inside the shoebox outside the gym before entering.

The parking area, near the northern gate, is available for use if there are any free spots.
